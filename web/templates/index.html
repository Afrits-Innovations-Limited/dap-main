Introducing Safe Mode

Risk reduction is our top priority. Now you can manage your transactions with one button, with our team of experts on hand to assist secure both buyers and sellers.

For Buyers

DAP offers you the ability to send money to anyone with confidence, in lieu of the product or services you are yet to receive, but payments made under escrow are not made available to sellers for spending until you release the funds to them.

For Sellers

With DAP you can transact with anyone from anywhere. Request an escrow payment from your customers who finds it difficult to trust you and deliver the services/products to them because DAP got you.

For DAP

As your dedicated fintech team, we act as an independent party to your transaction, holding assets in escrow and making sure they are disbursed quickly and securely according to the terms of the transaction agreement.
