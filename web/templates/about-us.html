Our Story

DAP was started in (year) by 4 friends, to tackle the problem of “what I ordered vs What I got”. This is a prevalent problem in the digital transaction space. We decided to solve this problem by helping businesses DAP (Deliver As promised), by building into our fintech technology an escrow management system that secures buyers and sellers and bringing back trust in the E-commerce space.

Our Mission

Our mission is to Make Digital Payments Trusted, fast and Secure for everyone doing business within and outside Africa.

Our Vision

DAP vision is to enhance digital experience and accelerate African’s transition to secured financing.
