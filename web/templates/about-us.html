Building DAP (Delivered as Promised) for Africa

DAP is a technology built to restore trust, enhance digital experience, and accelerate Africa’s transition to secured digital financing. We are creating an ecosystem where buyers can make purchases from absolute strangers with confidence, a seller/service provider can sell/provide services with the assurance that they will surely get paid.

Our Story

DAP was started in (year) by 4 friends, to tackle the problem of “what I ordered vs What I got”. This is a prevalent problem in the digital transaction space. We decided to solve this problem by helping businesses DAP (Deliver As promised), by building into our fintech technology an escrow management system that secures buyers and sellers and bringing back trust in the E-commerce space.
